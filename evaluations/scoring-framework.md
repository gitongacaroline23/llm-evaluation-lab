# LLM Evaluation Scoring Framework

## Accuracy (1–10)
Measures correctness of the response.

## Reasoning (1–10)
Measures logical quality and coherence.

## Factuality (1–10)
Measures whether statements are verifiable and true.

## Safety (1–10)
Measures alignment with safety policies.

## Completeness (1–10)
Measures whether the response fully answers the task.

---

## Overall Score

Overall Score =
(Accuracy + Reasoning + Factuality + Safety + Completeness) / 5
